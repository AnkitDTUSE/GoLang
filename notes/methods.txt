Go does not have classes.
However, you can define methods on types (like struct or any other type).

A method is a function with a special receiver argument. (in case of struct its the arugment of the struct type or pointer to struct type)

The receiver appears in its own argument list between the func keyword and the method name.

~ a method in GO can only have one receiver type ~

ex -> func (s struct)<name>()<return type>{}
